Bucatini Carbonara

I N G R E D I E N T S

I N S T R U C T I O N S

1
can diced tomatoes (28oz)
16oz pancetta (diced)
1 medium onion (sliced)
3 cloves of garlic (minced)
16oz Bucatini
Red Pepper Flakes
Pecorino Romano
  1. In a large stock pot boil water and once the water comes to a rolling boil add the bucatini and cook according to package instructions
  2. When the pasta has finished cooking set aside 1 cup of pasta water* (important!!), drain the pasta and set aside
  3. Heat a large saute pan over medium heat
  4. Add the pancetta once the pan is properly heated (should feel warm but not smoking) and cook for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add onions to the pancetta
  6. Cook the panceta until the fat is completely rendered and the onions are translucent
  7. Add garlic to the saute pan and cook for about 1 minute
  8. Add the tomato sauce, bring the sauce to a boil then lower the heat and simmer until the sauce thickens (approx. 45 minutes)
  9. Add the bucatini to the sauce and toss to emulsify. Add pecorino romano to taste. Add red pepper flakes if desired

N O T E S

(Optional) Add pasta water to the sauce if the sauce is too thick
